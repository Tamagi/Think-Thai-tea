Why
Think Thai?

Created from the heart of Chatuchak Market in Thailand, Thai Tea has been a staple in every day lives of individual Thais and international tourists.

Think Thai is bringing the concept of the authentic Thai Tea experience right here to share the freshness of the product. From the way we brew our tea to the way we serve them to our customers.

Our entire product making is shown live direct in front of the customers just as how it is like in the streets of Bangkok.

Our
Benefit

To be one of our franchisee we offer you the lowest investment with no royalty fee that guarantee you fast return on investment.

As one of the leading Thai Tea franchise brand in Indonesia, we are known for our innovative on our menu selections. To boost up quality of our service and products, our team ready to provide you national wide support and assistance.
